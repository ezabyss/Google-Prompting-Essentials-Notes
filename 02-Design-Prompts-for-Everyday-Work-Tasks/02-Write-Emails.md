# Using Generative AI to Write & Improve Emails

## Overview
Generative AI helps professionals write better emails by drafting content, adjusting tone, and adapting messages for different audiences. By using clear prompts and iterating on outputs, the same information can be reused while changing how the message sounds and who it is meant for.

## Real-World Problem
Writing effective emails is challenging because different audiences require different tones, important messages must be concise, drafting takes time, and follow-up emails often go unanswered. Generative AI acts as a drafting and editing assistant to reduce effort and improve clarity.

## What Generative AI Is Good At
- Drafting emails from scratch  
- Rewriting content for clarity  
- Adjusting tone and style  
- Adapting one message for multiple audiences  
- Generating creative variations  

## Prompt Framework
Use this repeatable structure when prompting generative AI:
1. Define the task  
2. Provide context or persona  
3. Specify the audience  
4. Clarify tone and format  
5. Evaluate and iterate  

## Prompt Example: Internal Staff Email
You are a gym manager.

> Write a short, professional, and friendly email informing staff about a new gym schedule.  
> Highlight that the Monday, Wednesday, and Friday Cardio Blast class has changed from 7:00 AM to 6:00 AM.  
> Keep the email concise and easy to skim.

## Prompt Example: Iteration for Gym Members
Same information, new audience, more motivating tone.

> Make it consumer-facing, concise, and engaging.

## Additional Prompt Examples

Follow-Up Email  
> Rewrite this follow-up email to sound polite, confident, and non-pushy.  
> Keep it under 100 words and professional.

Executive Audience  
> Rewrite this email for senior executives.  
> Make it formal, concise, and focused on outcomes.

Casual Team Message  
> Rewrite this message for a small internal team.  
> Make it friendly, casual, and supportive.

## Key Takeaways
Prompt clarity determines output quality. One message can be adapted for many audiences. Iteration improves tone and impact. Generative AI supports communication but does not replace human judgment.

## Best Practices
Always verify factual accuracy, be specific about audience and tone, iterate instead of accepting the first draft, and use AI as a drafting and refinement partner.
